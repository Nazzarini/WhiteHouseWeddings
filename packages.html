The Estate and the Barn/2 Days

This Ultimate Package includes the White House and a charming barn located just steps from the home for 2 days and 2 nights. The house is complete with a private Master Suite with adjoining bath & Jacuzzi, walk in shower, private toilet and Ladies Boudoir. Invite guest to stay and enjoy the three queen bedrooms, a chapel room, a Recreational Room, a Gentlemen's Quarters, a chic living area, a fully equipped kitchen, a baby grand piano, and the turn of the century dining area. All and more is yours for enjoyment within a spacious 4600 square foot Greek Revival Home.

The barn is a 2400 square foot fully encased knotty pine space. It is beautifully equipped with tables and chairs, a bar, 6 chandeliers, 3 ceiling fans, an elegant focal point and easily accommodates 60 guests. More tables and chairs can be rented to seat up to 100 guest. This Ultimate package is the perfect place to host your special day!
